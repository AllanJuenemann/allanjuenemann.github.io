Basic Jogger Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Basic Jogger app for iOS ("the app") by Allan Juenemann ("the developer").

No Personal Information Is Collected From the App

All data that you provide is stored only on your device. The developer does not collect, use, save, or has any access to your personal data recorded in the app. You might be asked to allow access to your motion data, but this is only so that your jogging distance can be displayed and stored by the app on your device.
